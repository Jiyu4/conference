Travel Information

By Air

Ninoy Aquino International Airport (MNL) is the main gateway. Several terminals serve international and domestic flights.

By Train

MRT and LRT provide easy access. North Avenue Station is closest to the venue (500m walk).

By Car

Located on EDSA (Epifanio de los Santos Avenue). Ride-sharing services (Grab) are readily available.
