Meet Our Experts

Keynote Speakers

Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Professor of Computer Science

Stanford University, USA

Leading expert in artificial intelligence and machine learning applications in education. Dr. Mitchell has published over 150 papers and is a recipient of the ACM Distinguished Scientist award.

Prof. James Chen

Director of Research

Tsinghua University, China

Renowned researcher in sustainable technology and green innovation systems. Prof. Chen has led multiple international research initiatives and serves as an advisor to governments on climate policy.

Dr. Maria Santos

UNESCO Education Specialist

UNESCO, France

International expert in educational policy and inclusive learning environments. Dr. Santos has been instrumental in developing global frameworks for quality education access.

Prof. David Okonkwo

Dean of Business School

University of Lagos, Nigeria

Distinguished scholar in strategic management and organizational leadership. Prof. Okonkwo has consulted for Fortune 500 companies and is a thought leader in emerging market strategies.
