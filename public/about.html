About the Conference

Learn more about IRECSTEM 2026

Welcome to IRECSTEM 2026

The 1st International Research and Extension Conference on Science, Technology, Education, and Management (IRECSTEM 2026) is a premier academic event that brings together researchers, educators, practitioners, and industry professionals from around the globe.

Our conference serves as a dynamic platform for sharing groundbreaking research, exchanging innovative ideas, and fostering meaningful collaborations across multiple disciplines. We believe that the convergence of diverse perspectives in science, technology, education, and management can lead to transformative solutions for global challenges.

At IRECSTEM 2026, participants will have the opportunity to engage with leading experts, present their own research, and contribute to shaping the future of these interconnected fields. Whether you are an established researcher, a doctoral student, or a practicing professional, our conference offers valuable insights and networking opportunities.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive international forum that promotes scholarly excellence, interdisciplinary collaboration, and the practical application of research findings in science, technology, education, and management.

Our Vision

To be a leading catalyst for global research advancement and knowledge exchange that addresses pressing societal challenges through innovative, cross-disciplinary approaches.

Goals

Conference Objectives

  • Foster Interdisciplinary Dialogue

    Create meaningful conversations between researchers from different disciplines to uncover novel approaches and solutions.

  • Promote Research Excellence

    Recognize and celebrate outstanding research contributions with awards and publication opportunities.

  • Bridge Academia and Industry

    Facilitate partnerships between academic researchers and industry practitioners for real-world impact.

  • Support Emerging Researchers

    Provide mentorship, guidance, and visibility for doctoral students and early-career researchers.
