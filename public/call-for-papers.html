Submit your research for presentation at IRECSTEM 2026

Call for Papers

We invite researchers, academics, and practitioners to submit papers on topics related to science, technology, education, and management. All accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings and will be indexed in major academic databases.

Paper Requirements:

  • Original research not previously published
  • 4-8 pages in IEEE/ACM format
  • Abstract (200-300 words) required
  • Minimum 3 keywords
  • All papers peer-reviewed

Important Notes:

  • At least one author must register and present the paper
  • Each registration covers one paper only
  • Authors may submit multiple papers
  • Best papers will be selected for special sessions
