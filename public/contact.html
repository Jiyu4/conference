Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the conference?

You can register online through our registration page. Select your registration type (Student, Regular, or Virtual) and complete the registration form.

What is the deadline for paper submission?

Abstract submissions are due by March 15, 2026. Full papers must be submitted by June 15, 2026.

Is there a refund policy?

Conference registration is non-refundable but is transferrable to another attendee if needed.

How can I become a sponsor?

Contact our sponsorship team via email or through the contact form with "Sponsorship" as the subject.
