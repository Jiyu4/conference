About the Conference

The 1st International Research and Extension Conference on Science, Technology, Education, and Management (IRECSTEM 2026) brings together researchers, educators, and industry professionals from around the world to share knowledge and advance interdisciplinary collaboration.

This premier event provides a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, exchanging ideas, and fostering partnerships that address global challenges in science, technology, education, and management domains.

Join us in September 2026 for an enriching experience that combines scholarly excellence with practical insights and networking opportunities.
